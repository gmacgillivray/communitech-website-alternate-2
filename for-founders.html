Your Founder Journey with Communitech

Here's what the typical founder experience looks like at Communitech.

Months 1-3: Validation

  • Join a founder program (Rev, Fierce Founders, or cohort)
  • Conduct 20-50 customer discovery interviews
  • Validate problem-solution fit
  • Build initial MVP or prototype
  • Attend workshops on lean startup methodology
  • Connect with mentors in your industry

Months 4-6: Launch

  • Launch MVP with first customers
  • Iterate based on feedback
  • Develop pricing and business model
  • Attend pitch coaching sessions
  • Begin fundraising conversations
  • Join founder circles for ongoing support

Months 7-12: Growth

  • Scale to 10-20 paying customers
  • Hire first employees (we'll help!)
  • Secure pre-seed or seed funding
  • Refine go-to-market strategy
  • Access corporate partner introductions
  • Consider transitioning to scale-up programs

Beyond Year 1

  • Graduate to scale-up programs
  • Become a mentor for new founders
  • Continue accessing office hours and workshops
  • Leverage Communitech network for hiring and partnerships
