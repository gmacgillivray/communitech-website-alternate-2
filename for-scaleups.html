Is This Right for Your Company?

Our scale-up programs are designed for companies that have achieved product-market fit and are ready to accelerate growth.

Ideal Profile

  • $500K+ annual revenue (or significant user traction)
  • Product-market fit demonstrated
  • Repeatable sales or acquisition motion
  • 5-50 employees
  • Seed funding secured or bootstrapped profitably
  • Ready to scale to new markets or segments
  • Based in Canada or expanding to Canada
