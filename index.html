About Communitech

Communitech is a founder-focused innovation hub based in Waterloo Region, helping startups and scale-ups build world-class technology companies and compete on the global stage.

Our Mission

We exist to make Canada the best place in the world to start, grow, and succeed in building a tech company. We connect founders with the people, programs, and partners they need at every stage of growth.

From early customer discovery to global expansion, Communitech supports founders with mentorship, capital access, global partners, and a vibrant peer community.

Who We Support

  • Early-stage founders validating their first customers
  • Scale-ups navigating rapid growth and global markets
  • Corporate innovators seeking startup partnerships
  • Investors, mentors, and ecosystem builders

Situated in the heart of Waterloo Region, we’re part of one of the world’s most dynamic tech ecosystems — anchored by research, talent, and a strong culture of collaboration.
